Bio

Bayside is an American emo punk band that was formed in the year 2000. Based in New York City, in the Bayside, Queens neighborhood the band has created an occult following using their slogan "Bayside is a Cult". One of the things that sets this band apart from the mainstream punk rockers is their use of acoustic instruments in a few of their song and albums as well as their accoustic tour which they will put on every so often.
